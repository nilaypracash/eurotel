Terms & Conditions

The house rules for staying with us — written plainly, the way we'd explain them over the front desk.

Last updated · July 2026

These terms govern all reservations and stays at Eurotel, Diu. We take bookings personally — by phone, WhatsApp, or email — and by confirming a reservation with us through any of these channels you accept the terms below.

01Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in: from 2:00 PM.
  • Check-out: before 10:00 AM.
  • Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.
  • Guests must present a valid government-issued photo ID upon check-in.

02Reservations

  • Reservations are confirmed only after receipt of the required payment or deposit.
  • Rates are subject to availability and may change without prior notice.
  • Special requests are subject to availability and cannot be guaranteed.

03Cancellations

  • Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival.
  • Cancellations made within 48 hours may be charged for one night's stay.
  • No-shows will be charged according to the booking conditions.

04Payment

Accepted payment methods: cash, bank transfer, and credit/debit cards (where available).

  • Full payment is required at check-in unless otherwise agreed.

05House Rules

Guests are expected to:

  • Respect other guests and neighbours.
  • Keep noise to a minimum, especially between 10:00 PM and 7:00 AM.
  • Keep the property clean and tidy.
  • Report any damage immediately.

06Smoking

  • Smoking is strictly prohibited inside all guest rooms.
  • Smoking is permitted only in designated outdoor areas.

07Alcohol & Illegal Drugs

  • Illegal drugs are strictly prohibited.
  • Excessive alcohol consumption causing disturbance is not permitted.

08Visitors

  • Visitors must be registered at reception.
  • Overnight visitors are not permitted without prior approval.
  • Additional charges may apply.

09Children

  • Children are welcome.
  • Parents or guardians are responsible for supervising children at all times.

10Pets

  • Pets are not allowed unless prior written approval has been obtained.

11Damage

  • Guests are responsible for any loss or damage caused to the property during their stay.
  • Repair or replacement costs will be charged accordingly.

12Safety & Security

  • Guests should lock doors and windows when leaving the room.
  • Eurotel is not responsible for loss of valuables left unattended.
  • Emergency contact information is available at reception.

13Lost & Found

  • Lost items found on the property will be kept for 30 days.
  • Shipping costs for returning items are the responsibility of the guest.

14Wi-Fi

  • Complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the property.
  • Guests must use the internet responsibly and in accordance with applicable laws.

15Privacy

  • Eurotel respects the privacy of all guests. Personal information is collected solely for reservation, legal registration, security, and service improvement purposes, and is handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

16Emergency Procedures

In the event of an emergency:

  • Remain calm.
  • Follow staff instructions.
  • Use emergency exits.
  • Do not use elevators during a fire.
  • Call reception immediately if assistance is required.

17Guest Conduct

Eurotel reserves the right to refuse accommodation or terminate a stay without refund if a guest:

  • Engages in abusive or threatening behaviour.
  • Damages property.
  • Violates house rules.
  • Disturbs other guests.
  • Participates in illegal activities.
