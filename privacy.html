Privacy Policy

What we collect when you enquire or stay, why we hold it, and the choices you have — in plain language.

Last updated · July 2026

Eurotel, Diu ("Eurotel", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you enquire with us or stay at the hotel, why we hold it, who we share it with, and the rights you have. For the purposes of India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, Eurotel is the Data Fiduciary responsible for your information.

01Information We Collect

When you make an enquiry (by our website form, WhatsApp, phone, or email):

  • Your name and contact details (phone number, email address).
  • Your requested stay details — check-in and check-out dates, number of guests, and anything you tell us in your message.

When you book and stay with us, and as required by law for lodging houses:

  • Government-issued photo identification (such as Aadhaar, passport, driving licence, or voter ID).
  • Residential address, nationality, and details recorded in the guest register.
  • For foreign nationals — passport and visa details required to file Form C with the immigration authorities (FRRO).
  • Payment details needed to process your advance, deposit, or final bill.

02How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data only for these purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry and confirm your reservation.
  • To provide and manage your stay, including check-in, billing, and guest services.
  • To meet legal and regulatory obligations — maintaining the guest register, filing Form C, and responding to lawful requests from police or authorities.
  • To keep the property and our guests safe and secure.
  • To contact you about your booking, and — only if you have agreed — about offers or news.

03Consent & Legal Basis

  • We process your data with your consent, given when you enquire or book with us.
  • Some data we are legally required to collect and retain (for example, guest-register and immigration records); this is processed to meet those legal obligations.
  • You may withdraw consent for non-essential processing (such as marketing) at any time by contacting us — this will not affect data we must keep by law.

04Who We Share It With

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share it only where necessary with:

  • Our own staff, to manage your enquiry and stay.
  • Payment providers and banks, to process payments.
  • Police, immigration, tax, or other authorities, where required by law.
  • Trusted service providers who help us operate (for example, our email host), under a duty of confidentiality.

05Third-party Services on Our Website

Our website is deliberately light and does not run advertising or tracking. It does use:

  • Google Fonts, which loads typefaces from Google's servers; your browser's IP address is visible to Google when fonts are fetched.
  • Mapbox, to display the interactive map on our Experiences page.
  • WhatsApp and email links, which open those apps to contact us — messages you send are then handled under WhatsApp's (Meta's) and your email provider's own privacy policies.

06Cookies & Local Storage

  • We do not use advertising or analytics cookies.
  • The site uses a small amount of temporary browser storage only to run the page-transition animation. It contains no personal data and is cleared automatically.

07How Long We Keep It

  • Enquiry messages that do not lead to a booking are kept only as long as needed to respond, then deleted.
  • Booking and guest-register records are retained for the period required by applicable hotel, tax, and immigration regulations, after which they are securely deleted or anonymised.

08How We Protect It

  • Access to guest information is limited to staff who need it to do their job.
  • Physical records are kept secure at the property, and digital records are protected with reasonable safeguards.
  • If a data breach affecting your rights occurs, we will act and notify as required by law.

09Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access a summary of the personal data we hold about you and how it is processed.
  • Ask us to correct or update inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Ask us to erase data we are no longer required to keep.
  • Nominate another person to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity.
  • Raise a grievance with us, and escalate to the Data Protection Board of India if unresolved.

10Children's Data

  • We collect a child's details only as part of a booking made by a parent or guardian, and only as needed for the stay and legal registration.
  • We do not knowingly use children's data for marketing or profiling.

11Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this policy from time to time. The version published on this page is the one that applies, and we will update the date above when we make changes.
